Featured Projects

Rewinder Accuracy Root-Cause Investigation

Challenge: Production rewinders were reporting inconsistent tape lengths, affecting measurement reliability across process steps.

Engineering: Verified PLC distance logic, hardware assumptions, encoder pulse consistency, and reel behavior using an independent vision-based check before isolating the true physical cause.

Result: Identified top-wheel effective diameter variation as the root cause of systematic length error and produced the technical evidence used for supplier-side corrective action.
